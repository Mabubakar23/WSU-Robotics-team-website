We share a common interest in problem solving, particularly solving issues with the aid of robotic systems.

Team Projects

You can find a list of all our current and past projects along with descriptions listed below. Feel free to also checkout our Youtube Channel!

Joy (Ground Rover)

Semi-Autonomous ground rover developed for the international ground vehicle competition 2019. Features a visual processing unit and a robust sensor suite. Capable of simultaneous localizing and mapping of both indoor and outdoor environments.

Veronica (Ground Rover)

Veronica is a second generation fully autonomous ground rover developed by WSU robotics. She is capable of unmanned navigation, obstacle avoidance, mapping, and has a top speed of 5 mph. Her GPS system is capable of localizing within 2.5cm anywhere on the globe. She is currently being developed and is expected to be fully operational by June 1st, 2020.
